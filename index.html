Title:
Biological Treatment of I.Wastewater
Definition: Wastewater from industrial activities. Importance of Treatment: Protects ecosystems, complies with laws, and safeguards health.
LUNDEV
What is Biological Wastewater Treatment?
Definition
Uses microorganisms to degrade pollutants naturally. Goal: Converts contaminants into harmless byproducts like water, carbon dioxide, and biomass.
LUNDEV
Why Choose Biological Treatment?
Reduces use of harmful chemicals.
Cost-Effective: Often cheaper than chemical treatments. Efficient: Capable of breaking down organic matter and some inorganic compounds.

Basic Principles of Biological Treatment
Aerobic ,Aerobic & Facultative Systems
Work in both aerobic and anaerobic conditions
